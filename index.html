Money Talk

Financing P2P Business with Facebook Messenger

A Facebook Chat Bot to Make Daily Transaction Easier With Friends and Strangers

Step 1 : Facebook Verification

Step 2 : Fill in Blue Bank Customer Number

Money Talk is a chatbot that allows you to check your bank account in Facebook Messenger. You can transfer money to friends and pay your purchase in the market. Our mission to promote better engagement with the customers with an easy-to-use tool, saving them from the hassles of notes and coins in daily transactions.
